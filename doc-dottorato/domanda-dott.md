# PhD Questions

- Explain your motivation for embarking on a thesis, and in particular for your chosen subject. What do you expect from training through research?

I consider myself a very curious person, eager to explore as much as I can
about the things that fascinate me. I believe a PhD thesis is the ideal
opportunity for me to fulfill my desire to learn and discover something new.
Since I was a child, I’ve been drawn to the world of computers, and as I
grew older, I became fascinated by the abstract world behind everything
digital.

Through my studies, I was introduced to algorithms and distributed systems,
which immediately captured my interest. The more complex the context, the
more I find the proposed solutions elegant and effective. That is exactly
why I believe this opportunity is a perfect fit for me.
